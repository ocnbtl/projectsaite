# Wardrobe Prompt Specifications

These specifications inherit the global blocks in ../../07-image-prompts.md. Garments remain PROPOSED until Sage approves silhouette, coverage, professional fit, movement, and downstream use.

## WARD-RUNWAY-001 — black architectural runway turnaround

1. Asset/purpose: unbranded runway garment system and transition fabric.
2. Inputs/controls: approved body/identity sheets control model; owner-approved material/silhouette references control garment only.
3. Identity lock: CHR-SAGE-CONT-001; garment may not reshape body.
4. Scene: neutral atelier/studio turnaround.
5. Placement/scale: full-body front, rear, left/right profile, three-quarter, plus hem/sleeve/fabric details.
6. Performance: neutral stance and controlled walking/fabric-sweep poses; hands visible where possible; focused expression.
7. Wardrobe: refined black architectural tailoring, opaque, structurally plausible, professional coverage, safe hardware, fabric mass sufficient for lens occlusion.
8. Camera: fixed level long-natural lens, consistent distance/height, f/8-like depth, crisp garment detail plus one documented directional-motion study.
9. Output: high-resolution individual views/contact sheet; no website crop.
10. Geometry: neutral floor grid and matched subject scale.
11. Light: soft neutral key plus edge light separating black material; accurate black detail and exposure.
12. Palette/grade: neutral black/graphite; no crushed diagnostic detail in turnaround.
13. Layers: subject/garment/floor only; detail views isolate seams, hem, sleeves, fabric.
14. Continuity: garment version is fixed for KF-01–03; occluding panel direction maps to TRANS-OCCLUSION-001.
15. Preserve: body/identity, approved neckline, length, sleeve, closure, hem, fabric weight, shoe.
16. Prevent: logos, recognizable trade dress, text, transparent cloth, clipping, fused hands, floating hem, unsafe hard object near lens.
17. Output requirements: six turnaround views, four details, two movement studies, exact material notes.
18. Downstream: runway keyframes, fabric motion test, occlusion plan.
19. Review: fit/professional appropriateness, anatomy, construction, motion, black coverage, brand clearance.
20. Provenance/rights/state: source-linked where references exist; no brand reproduction; private PROPOSED until approved.

## WARD-RESORT-001 — warm-neutral resort turnaround

1. Asset/purpose: unbranded resort look for reveal and resolve.
2. Inputs/controls: approved body/identity sheets; owner-approved silhouette/material references control garment only.
3. Identity lock: CHR-SAGE-CONT-001; wardrobe cannot alter proportions or skin tone.
4. Scene: neutral daylight-balanced atelier/studio.
5. Placement/scale: full-body front, rear, profiles, three-quarter, movement, fabric and closure details.
6. Performance: neutral stance, walking, settle, and approved glance-back; hands visible; expression source-bounded.
7. Wardrobe: refined ivory/warm-neutral linen-inspired look, opaque, movement-safe, professional public-portfolio styling, compatible with resort breeze.
8. Camera: same diagnostic geometry as body sheet, natural long lens, sufficient depth, crisp fabric detail.
9. Output: high-resolution individual views/contact sheet; no website crop.
10. Geometry: fixed floor plane/grid and subject scale.
11. Light: neutral daylight with highlight protection on pale fabric and accurate skin exposure.
12. Palette/grade: ivory, warm neutral, restrained natural accent; no orange cast.
13. Layers: subject/garment/floor; detail views isolate weave, hem, closure, movement.
14. Continuity: garment fixed from KF-04–06; hidden-cut transformation from runway look is documented, not morphed on screen.
15. Preserve: identity/body, approved silhouette, opacity, length, closure, footwear, fabric weight.
16. Prevent: logos, text, transparency, wardrobe malfunction, tropical cliché print, clipping, changed body, floating fabric.
17. Output requirements: six turnaround views, four details, movement/breeze studies.
18. Downstream: resort keyframes, motion, poster.
19. Review: professional styling, fit, anatomy, material physics, highlight recovery, brand clearance.
20. Provenance/rights/state: source-linked where applicable; private PROPOSED until Sage/owner approval.
