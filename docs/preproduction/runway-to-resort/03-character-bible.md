## Allowed variation

- Expression within approved expression-sheet range.
- Runway-to-resort wardrobe, light, and environment transformation at the hidden cut.
- Hair motion consistent with the approved hair sheet and environmental force.
- Pose/gait phase that remains physically adjacent to neighboring frames.
- Minor grade/exposure adjustments that do not change skin tone or features.

## Prohibited drift

- Face averaging, face swap artifacts, mixed apparent age, eye-color changes, changed hairline, or altered distinguishing features.
- Height, limb, shoulder, waist, hand, foot, or head-to-body changes between frames.
- Skin-lightening/darkening caused by inconsistent grading rather than physical lighting.
- Unapproved makeup, tattoos, jewelry, logos, text, or wardrobe details.
- Extra/missing fingers, teeth artifacts, fused limbs, floating fabric, heel/floor mismatch, or impossible gait.
- Sudden screen-direction reversal or teleporting subject scale.

## Review protocol

1. Compare every output against all thirteen original photographs. Use matched crops for close facial review without treating the crop as a replacement training source.

4. Review hands, feet, teeth, ears, hairline, jewelry, and garment edges at 100%.
5. Compare adjacent frames side by side and as a rapid A/B loop.

7. Record each defect in the manifest; do not hide it in chat history.
